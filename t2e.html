Logo Design: Talents 2 Europe

This is definitely my favorite project i have ever done. Talents To Europe is a football agency based in Portugal. It’s goal is to scout young talents from Egypt to Portugal. My approach when designing this logo was to create an icon. Since the agency’s name: Talents to Europe, is quite long i decided to abbreviate it to T2E and create a symbol or icon out of it. I created negative space out of the letters in order to create the shape you see. The colors i chose also combine the Egyptian and Portugese flags.
