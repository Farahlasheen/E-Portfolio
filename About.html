About Me

My name is Farah Lasheen. I am 19 years old. I am a designer, artist, musician, and student. I study Digital media at Coventry University. I specialise in Graphic Design and working with illustrator. I love to sketch and draw. My portfolio consists of logo design, and hand drawn art pieces. My interest in art and design started when I was a little girl. Creativity is one of the most crucial aspects in all of my designs.
